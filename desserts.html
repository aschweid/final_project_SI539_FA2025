Dessert Recipes

Oreo Truffles

  1. Get 1 regular pack of Oreos (~36 cookies) and 8 oz of cream cheese
  2. Blend up or smash oreos into a fine crumb
  3. Mix or beat Oreo crumbs wiht the cream cheese until combined
  4. Roll balls of the mixture and refridgerate for at least 1 hour
  5. Melt 12 oz of chocolate with 1/2 tsp of vegetable oil
  6. Let chocolate cool a little and dip oreo balls
  7. Refridgerate truffles for at least 1 hour

Chocolate Chip Cookies

  1. Combine 2 and 1/4 cups of flour, 1 tsp of baking soda and 1 tsp of salt in a bowl
  2. Beat 2 sticks of butter, 3/4 cup of brown sugar, 3/4 cup of granulated sugar and 1 tsp of vanilla extract until creamy
  3. Add 2 eggs to the butter-sugar mixture 1 at a time
  4. Add in flour mixture
  5. Stir in ~2 cups of chocolate chips
  6. If the dough is soft, refridgerate for ~30 minutes
  7. Roll cookies and place onto a baking sheet
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown at 375* F
