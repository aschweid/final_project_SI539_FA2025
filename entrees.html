Entree Recipes

Lemon chicken

  1. Slice chicken breasts thin
  2. Dredge chicken lightly in flour mixed with a little salt and pepper
  3. Heat olive oil and little butter together
  4. Brown chicken on both sides then remove from pan
  5. Scrape pan to loosens the brown bits
  6. Add ~1tbsp of butter, melt it, and add lemon juice to taste
  7. Add 1/2 cup to 1 cup chicken broth and 1/2 tsp flour
  8. Stir to make sauce thicker and bring to a boil
  9. Turn down heat, add chicken back and add parsley
  10. Serve over orzo or egg noodles

Chicken Lettuce Wraps

  1. Whisk together 3 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp brown sugar, 1.5 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tbsp of chili garlic sauce, salt and pepper in a bowl
  2. Set aside some of the sauce for later
  3. Cut chicken into thin strips and add them to the marinade
  4. If time, let the chicken sit in the marinade for a few hours
  5. Heat oil to medium-high heat in a pan and cook chicken
  6. Add chicken to butter lettuce leaves and top with slicked cucumber, green onions and cilantro
  7. Heat remaining sauce on the stove to thicken and drizzle over the lettuce wraps

Chicken Cutlets

  1. Slice chicken breasts thin
  2. Mix 1 egg, a little milk, pepper and some parmesan cheese
  3. Add chicken and let soak for 15 mins to 1 hour
  4. Brown chicken on both sides then remove from pan
  5. Coat chicken in Italian bread crumbs
  6. Fry chicken in a pan with olive oil 3-5 mins per side until brown

Grilled Cheese

  1. Heat butter in pan
  2. Assemble sandwich with choice of cheese
  3. Cook and flip until golden brown on each side
  4. Serve with potato chips if desired

One Pan Pasta

  1. Add olive oil to a pan on medium-high heat
  2. Add minced garlic and your pasta of choice
  3. Coat the pasta in the oil and add 2 tbsp of salted butter
  4. Pour in chicken broth to fully cover pasta
  5. Bring pasta to a boil until the broth is absorbed
  6. Add tomato paste and a half cup of heavy cream
  7. Mix well and add a big pinch of salt
  8. Top with parsely and parmesan cheese

Chicken Stir Fry

  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Coat chicken with a little flour mixed with salt and pepper
  3. Cook chicken in olive oil on the stove
  4. Remove chicken from pan and add in fresh or frozen veggies of choice
  5. Cook veggies till tender
  6. Add stir fry sauce to center of the pan
  7. Stir to make sauce thicker and bring to a boil
  8. Add chicken back in and mix all together
  9. Serve with rice

Homemade Chipotle Bowls

  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Coat chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, chile powder, garlic powder and chipotle seasoning
  3. Cook chicken in olive oil on the stove
  4. Cook rice and add in cilantro and lime juice to taste when finished
  5. Combine with a choice of guacamole, salsa, cheese etc.

Italian Sausage Pasta

  1. Put one pack of italian sausage in a pot and cook on medium heat
  2. Break up the sausage into bite sized pieces as it cooks
  3. Separately cook pasta accoding to the directions on the box
  4. Add in tomato sauce to the sausage and heat it up
  5. Season sauce with basil, garlic powder, red pepper flakes and salt
  6. When pasta is done, add it to the sauce and serve
  7. Top with fresh basil and parmesan if desired

Chicken and Pineapple Curry

  1. Cut 1-2 chicken breasts into cubes
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper, paprika and garlic powder
  3. Cook chicken in olive oil on the stove then set aside
  4. Using the same pan on medium heat add minced garlic and 2 tbsp of red curry paste and mix with the leftover oil
  5. Add in 3/4 of a can of coconut milk, diced bell pepper and carrots cut into thin sticks
  6. Cook until veggetables are tender, then add ~1 cup of cubed pineapple
  7. Bring the curry to a simmer and season with salt to taste
  8. Stir in chicken and chopped cilantro
  9. Remove from heat and serve with rice and extra cilantro

Salmon Bite Bowl

  1. Cube salmon and remove any skin and bones
  2. Combine 2 tbsp of soy sauce, 2 tsp of honey, 1 tsp of rice vinegar, 1/2 tsp of sesame oil, garlic and ginger in a bowl
  3. Marinate salmon bites for 10 minutes in the mixture
  4. Cook salmon cubes on medium-high heat for 5-7 minutes and pour leftover marinade over the salmon
  5. Assemble bowls with rice, salmon, cucumber, carrots, avocado and edamame

Homemade Pizza

  1. Pick up pre made pizza dough from Kroger or Trader Joes, or make your own
  2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or spray with cooking spray
  3. Spread dough out into a circle
  4. Top with your choice of tomato sauce, seasonings, cheese and other toppings
  5. Bake at 425* F for 15 minutes or until fully cooked

One Pan Italian Sausage and Vegetables

  1. Line a large sheet pan with parchment paper
  2. Cut carrots and red bell pepper into slices
  3. Cut brocoli into florets so they are about the same size as the other vegetables
  4. Cut sausage into small coins or cubes
  5. Put all vegetables and sausage onto the sheet pan and drizzle with olive oil
  6. Season with salt, pepper, italian seasoning, garlic powder and red pepper flakes
  7. Toss vegetables and sausage and space out everything so there is room to roast
  8. Bake at 400* F for 15 minutes, then flip everything and bake for another 10 minutes
  9. Serve with rice or orzo if desired
