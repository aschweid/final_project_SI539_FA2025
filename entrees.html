Entree Recipes

Lemon chicken

  1. Slice chicken breasts thin
  2. Dredge chicken lightly in flour mixed with a little salt and pepper
  3. Heat olive oil and little butter together
  4. Brown chicken on both sides then remove from pan
  5. Scrape pan to loosens the brown bits
  6. Add ~1tbsp of butter, melt it, and add lemon juice to taste
  7. Add 1/2 cup to 1 cup chicken broth and 1/2 tsp flour
  8. Stir to make sauce thicker and bring to a boil
  9. Turn down heat, add chicken back and add parsley
  10. Serve over orzo or egg noodles

Chicken Cutlets

  1. Slice chicken breasts thin
  2. Mix 1 egg, a little milk, pepper and some parmesan cheese
  3. Add chicken and let soak for 15 mins to 1 hour
  4. Brown chicken on both sides then remove from pan
  5. Coat chicken in Italian bread crumbs
  6. Fry chicken in a pan with olive oil 3-5 mins per side until brown

Grilled Cheese

  1. Heat butter in pan
  2. Assemble sandwich with choice of cheese
  3. Cook and flip until golden brown on each side
  4. Serve with potato chips if desired

Chicken Stir Fry

  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Coat chicken with a little flour mixed with salt and pepper
  3. Cook chicken in olive oil on the stove
  4. Remove chicken from pan and add in fresh or frozen veggies of choice
  5. Cook veggies till tender
  6. Add stir fry sauce to center of the pan
  7. Stir to make sauce thicker and bring to a boil
  8. Add chicken back in and mix all together
  9. Serve with rice

Homemade Chipotle Bowls

  1. Cut chicken into cubes
  2. Coat chicken with salt, pepper, paprika,chile powder, garlic powder and chipotle seasoning
  3. Cook chicken in olive oil on the stove
  4. Cook rice and add in cilantro and lime juice to taste when finished
  5. Combine with a choice of guacamole, salsa, cheese etc.
