Side Dishes and Sauces

Mom’s Homemade Tomato Sauce

  1. Get 2 cans of whole peeled tomatoes (tuttarosa or rienzi)
  2. Optional: Put peeled tomatoes in blender and purée for 10 seconds (1 can at a time)
  3. Cover the base of the pot in a layer of olive oil
  4. Add 3 cloves of garlic per can of tomatoes
  5. Add tomatoes (turn of heat and use splash guard/pot lid)
  6. Add basil, 2-3 red peppers and 1.5 tablespoon of salt
  7. Cover with splash guard, set a timer for 12 minutes and cook on medium high heat
  8. Drop to medium heat for 12-15 minutes
  9. Serve over your favorite pasta noodle

Kale chips

  1. Get a bag of chopped kale or chop your own
  2. Cover sheet pan in tin foil or parchment paper and place kale chips on it
  3. Drizzle olive oil, salt and pepper onto kale
  4. Optional: sprinkle red pepper flakes
  5. Bake at 350*F for ~15 minutes or until at the desired crispiness level

Crispy Seasoned Potatoes

  1. Chop red potatoes (or other potato of choice) into small cubes
  2. Place potatoes on a tin foil or parchment paper covered pan and drizzle with olive oil
  3. Season with salt and pepper then add based on preferences
  4. Seasoning options:
    • Montreal Steak Seasoning
    • Garlic powder and rosemary
    • Italian seasoning
  5. Toss potatoes to distribute the seasoning evenly
  6. Bake at 400*F for ~25 minutes or until potatoes are cooked through and crispy

Oven Baked Carrots

  1. Place baby carrots on a tin foil or parchment paper lined baking sheet
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper and garlic powder
  3. Top with italian breadcrumbs
  4. Bake at 400* for ~15-20 minutes or until tender

Pineapple Salsa

  1. Cube a small container or ~2/3 cup of pineapple
  2. Chop 1/2 of a bell pepper, 1/4 of a red onion and 3 tbsp of cilantro
  3. Mix together in a bowl
  4. Add 2 tbsp of lime juice and a pinch of salt
  5. Serve with chips or in a burrito bowl

Rice

  1. Wash 1 cup of rice in a pot well
  2. Add 1.5 cups of water and a pinch of salt
  3. Bring rice to a boil
  4. Cover, lower the heat and simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Remove from heat and let sit still covered for 10 minutes
  6. Fluff rice with a fork
